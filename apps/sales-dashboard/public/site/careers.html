What we're like

Three things we optimise for.

01

Ship today.

We don't have a staging environment religion. We have a "did it close more pitches today" religion.

02

Close the feedback loop.

Every decision comes back to the contractor on the patch. If a change doesn't help them, it doesn't ship.

03

Small team, long calendar.

We'd rather hire slowly and well. Nobody is paged at 3am to fix someone else's opinion.
