Plain English: we use a small number of strictly-necessary cookies. We don't use advertising or tracking cookies. You can turn the optional ones off below.

01What cookies are.

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device. Some are necessary for the site to work; others are optional.

02What we use.

We use three categories: strictly-necessary (login, CSRF), preferences (language, theme), and aggregate analytics (privacy-preserving; no cross-site tracking).
