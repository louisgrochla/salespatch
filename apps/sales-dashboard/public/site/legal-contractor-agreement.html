Contractor agreement.

Last updated · [TBD] · Plain-English summary first, terms below
Plain English: you are a self-employed contractor. You decide when and where you work. You get £50 on signature, no clawback. We provide the app, the leads, the demos, and the payments rails. You provide the walking and the talking. Neither of us is the other's employer.

01Your status.

You are a self-employed contractor and not an employee, worker, or agent of SalesFlow. You are responsible for your own tax and National Insurance. We provide a [TBD] annual earnings statement; self-assessment is your responsibility.

02The services.

You agree to use the platform to identify and engage small-business prospects and, where appropriate, close sales of the SalesFlow website product. No minimum hours. No territory exclusivity beyond an active claim window.

03Commission.

  • £50 per successful close, paid on signature.
  • Credited to your wallet within seconds of confirmed signature.
  • No clawback, including if the client later cancels.
  • Withdrawable to a UK bank account, usually within [TBD] hours.

04Your obligations.

  • Act lawfully and honestly. No misrepresentation of the product.
  • Respect the business owner's time and premises.
  • Log outcomes accurately in the app.
  • Pass change requests to the orchestration desk, not handle them yourself.

05Our obligations.

  • Provide the app, the lead queue, and demo generation.
  • Operate the orchestration desk for post-sale support.
  • Pay commission on signature, no clawback, no caveats.
  • Make statements and records available for your self-assessment.

06Data & privacy.

See our Privacy policy. Your activity data belongs to you; you can export it at any time.

07Termination.

Either party may end this agreement at any time with [TBD] days' notice. Wallet balances remain payable. We may suspend immediately for breach (fraud, abuse, misrepresentation).

08IP & confidentiality.

Pitch techniques and general know-how are yours. Platform internals, client lists, and generator specifics are confidential.

09Dispute resolution.

We try to resolve informally first. Failing that, English law applies; disputes are subject to the courts of England and Wales.
